About Me

With a lifelong passion for innovation and the cutting edge, my journey in technology began as a young coder, crafting unique game features that pushed boundaries. This early fascination has evolved into a successful career, where I've developed transformative software solutions that have saved companies significant time and money.

My experience spans the full life cycle of design and development, with a particular focus on complex websites. Recently, I've expanded my skill set to include DevOps, Next.js, and GraphQL, further enhancing my capabilities as a developer.

My greatest interest lies in development, specifically as a frontend or fullstack developer. I'm particularly intrigued by the potential of AI technologies like GPT-4 and Midjourney, and I'm eager to explore how these tools can revolutionize the development process.

As I continue to hone my skills and delve deeper into the realm of AI, I look forward to creating more efficient, dynamic, and innovative solutions that drive progress and redefine the technological landscape.
